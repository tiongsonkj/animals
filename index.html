My Favorite Animal

Wolf

The wolf is my favorite animal because of the way they interact with each other. Wolves are known for their howling at night. Wolves are always traveling in packs. Most importantly, they hunt together.

"The strength of the pack is the wolf and the strength of the pack is the wolf."
